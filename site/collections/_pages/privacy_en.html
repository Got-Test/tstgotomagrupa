PRIVACY POLICY

For your convenience GOTOMA Group website service uses cookies and similar technologies in order to tailor the service to users’ needs and for statistical purposes. Cookies are small text files sent by an internet service visited by the internet user to the internet user’s device.

You can change your cookie settings in your web browser. No change to these settings will constitute acceptance of the cookies used here.

The GOTOMA Group service uses performance cookies, which are used to gather information about how the site can better function, in the form of: 1. Cookies google-analytics.com – statistics for www.gotoma.pl. 2. Cache, for website load optimization. 3. Etag, for website load optimization. 4. To indicate that a cookie policy has been accepted.

The level of protection against cookies can be set in your web browser – until the cookies are completely blocked. This increases the level of security and data protection, but it can also prevent certain features, such as logging into your mail account.
